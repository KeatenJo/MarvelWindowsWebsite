About Us

Marvel Glass Cleaning was originally founded by twin brothers attempting to save money for their futures. As the business began to expand, it became apparent to these brothers that Marvel Glass Cleaning could reach out and help other individuals achieve their goals, too. Marvel’s overarching goal is to help young adults advance in education while providing our clients with the best service available, anywhere. Marvel Glass Cleaning has laid its roots in paying employees top dollar and expecting top results.

Our Services

Glass Cleaning:

Many people pay top dollar for a beautiful home with a beautiful view. We want to showcase your investment by clearing away the dust, dirt, hard water, and debris that dulls your home’s sparkling windows. We offer one-time services as well as packages for multiple visits per year. Have that beautiful view once again with one quick call.

Hard Water Removal:

Here in Utah the hard water buildup can cloud up windows and potentially cause lasting damage to the glass, but with regular cleaning it will keep the window looking as good as ever. Our hard water removal services are gentle and thorough. You won’t believe the difference Marvel Glass Cleaning can make!
